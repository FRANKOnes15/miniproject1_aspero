La Paz Batchoy

La Paz Batchoy is considered one of the most iconic dishes in Iloilo and the whole country. This exquisitely delicious noodle soup consists of pork meat, chicharon, egg noodles, flavorful broth, and raw egg as the main ingredients.

This extraordinary dish originated from the small town of La Paz, Iloilo, and is characterized by an oozing savory and delicious “liquid gold” soup.
