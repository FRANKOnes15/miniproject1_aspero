What is Byahero Iloilo? Explore Iloilo is a destination guide about travel and socially relevant developments in Iloilo and Western Visayas Region in the Philippines.

my mission is to inform and inspire people about the multifaceted potentials of Iloilo and the rest of the region.to fill the need for the promotion

of Iloilo to the global audience and bring about change in the Iloilo community by making locals and potential visitors realize that Iloilo has a lot to offer.

this blog and guides created for miniprject-1 and to connect the global community by helpful information on travel, real estate, and exciting developments happening in Iloilo and Western Visayas.
